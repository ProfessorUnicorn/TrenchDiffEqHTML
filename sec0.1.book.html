0.1 Prealgebra
Fractions
Complex Fractions: \[ \displaystyle\frac{\big(A\big)}{\left(\frac{Top}{Bottom}\right)} =\big(A\big) \div \left(\frac{Top}{Bottom}\right) \]
Divide Fractions: \[ \displaystyle\big(A\big) \div \left(\frac{Top}{Bottom}\right) = \big(A\big)x \cdot \left(\frac{Bottom}{Top}\right) \]
Multiply Fractions: \[ \displaystyle\big(A\big) \cdot \left(\frac{Bottom}{Top}\right) = \left(\frac{A}{1}\right) \cdot \left(\frac{Bottom}{Top}\right) = \frac{A \cdot Bottom}{1 \cdot Top} \]
Common Denom: \[ \displaystyle \frac{1st}{B} +\frac{2nd}{D} = \frac{1st}{B} \left( \frac{D}{D} \right) + \left( \frac{B}{B}\right) \frac{2nd}{D} = \frac{\left(1st \cdot D\right)+\left(2nd \cdot B\right)}{BD} \]
Flip Fractions: \[ \displaystyle \left(\frac{Top}{Bottom}\right)^{-1} = \left(\frac{Bottom}{Top}\right) \\ \displaystyle \frac{1}{Bottom^{-1}} = \frac{Bottom}{1} \\ \displaystyle \frac{Top^-1}{Bottom} = \frac{1}{Top \cdot Bottom} \]

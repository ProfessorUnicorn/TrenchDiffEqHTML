CHAPTER 6
Applications of Linear Second Order Equations

IN THIS CHAPTER we study applications of linear second order equations.

SECTION 6.1 is about spring–mass systems.

SECTION 6.2 is about \(RLC\) circuits, the electrical analogs of spring–mass systems.

SECTION 6.3 is about motion of an object under a central force, which is particularly relevant in the space age, since, for example, a satellite moving in orbit subject only to Earth’s gravity is experiencing motion under a central force.

10.

(a)

If \(s_1\), \(s_2\), …, \(s_n\) are distinct and \(P\) is a polynomial of degree less than \(n\), then \[ {P(s)\over (s-s_1)(s-s_2)\cdots (s-s_n)}= {A_1\over s-s_1}+{A_2\over s-s_2}+\cdots +{A_n\over s-s_n}\] Multiply through by \(s-s_i\) to show that \(A_i\) can be obtained by ignoring the factor \(s-s_i\) on the left and setting \(s=s_i\) elsewhere.

(b)

Suppose \(P\) and \(Q_1\) are polynomials such that \(\mbox {degree}(P)\le \mbox {degree}(Q_1)\) and \(Q_1(s_1)\ne 0\). Show that the coefficient of \(1/(s-s_1)\) in the partial fraction expansion of \[ F(s)={P(s)\over (s-s_1)Q_1(s)} \] is \(P(s_1)/Q_1(s_1)\).

(c)

Explain how the results of (a) and (b) are related.

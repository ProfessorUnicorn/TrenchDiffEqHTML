0.3 Trigonometry
Trig Definitions
Right Triangles: \( \sin\left(\theta\right)= \frac{opp}{hyp}=\frac{y}{r} \), \( \cos\left(\theta\right)= \frac{adj}{hyp}=\frac{x}{r} \), \( \tan\left(\theta\right)= \frac{opp}{adj}=\frac{y}{x} \)
Pythagorean: \( a^2+b^2=c^2 \), \( adj^2+opp^2=hyp^2 \), \( \cos^2{x}+\sin^2{x}=1 \)
Quotient: \( \tan\left(\theta\right)= \frac{\sin\left(\theta\right)}{\cos\left(\theta\right)} \), \( \cot\left(\theta\right)= \frac{\cos\left(\theta\right)}{\sin\left(\theta\right)} \)
Reciprocal: \( \csc\left(\theta\right)= \frac{1}{\sin\left(\theta\right)} \), \( \sec\left(\theta\right)= \frac{1}{\cos\left(\theta\right)} \), \( \cot\left(\theta\right)= \frac{1}{\tan\left(\theta\right)} \)
Inverse: \( \arcsin x = \sin^{-1} x \), \( \arccos x = \cos^{-1} x \), \( \arctan x = \tan^{-1} x \)
Even/Odd: \( \sin\left(-\theta\right)= -\sin\left(\theta\right) \), \( \cos\left(-\theta\right)= \cos\left(\theta\right) \)
Multiply Trig Functions:
\( f(x) \cdot f(x) = \left[f(x)\right]^2 \ne f(x^2) \)
\( \sin(x) \cdot \sin(x) = \left[\sin(x)\right]^2 = \sin^2(x) \ne \sin(x^2) \)
Trig Identities
Trig Addition:
\( \sin(A+B)=\sin(A)\cos(B)+\sin(B)\cos(A) \)
\( \cos(A+B)=\cos(A)\cos(B)-\sin(A)\sin(B) \)
Pythagorean: \( \sin^2(\theta) + \cos^2(\theta) = 1 \)
\( \sin^2(\theta) = 1 - \cos^2(\theta) \)
\( \cos^2(\theta) = 1 - \sin^2(\theta) \)
Double Angle:
\( \sin\left(2x\right) = 2\sin x \cos x \)
\( \cos\left(2x\right) = \cos^2 x - \sin^2 x \)
\( \cos\left(2x\right) = 2\cos^2 x - 1 \)
\( \cos\left(2x\right) = 1 - 2\sin^2 x \)
Half Angle:
\( \cos^2 x = \frac{1 + \cos \left(2x\right)}{2} \)
\( \sin^2 x = \frac{1 - \cos \left(2x\right)}{2} \)

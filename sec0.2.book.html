0.2 Algebra
Exponents
Definitions: \( x^0=1 \), \( x=x^1 \), \( \sqrt{x}=x^\frac{1}{2} \), \( x^\frac{2}{3} = \left(\sqrt[3]{x}\right)^2 \), \( \displaystyle \frac{1}{x}=x^{-1} \)
Multiply Variables: \( X^2 = X \cdot X \)
Add Exponents: \( X^2 \cdot X^3 = \left( X \cdot X \right) \cdot \left( X \cdot X \cdot X \right) = X^5 =X^{2+3} \)
Subtract Exponents: \( \displaystyle \frac{X^5}{X^8} = \frac{X \cdot X \cdot X \cdot X \cdot X}{X \cdot X \cdot X \cdot X \cdot X \cdot X \cdot X} =\frac{1}{x^3} =X^-3 =X^{5-8} \)
Multiply Exponents: \( \left(X^3\right)^2 =X^3 \cdot X^3 =\left(X \cdot X \cdot X\right) \left(X \cdot X \cdot X\right) =X^6 =X^{2\cdot3} \)
Logs
Natural Log: \( \ln x = \log_e x \approx \log_{2.71828} x \)
Change of Base: \( \displaystyle \log_{B}{A} = \frac{\ln A}{\ln B} =\left(\frac{1}{\ln B}\right)\ln A \)
Log Properties
Power: \( \log {x}^{power} = power \cdot \log{x} \)
Product: \( \log \left(1st \cdot 2nd\right) = \log \left(1st\right) + \log \left(2nd\right) \)
Quotient: \( \log \left(\frac{top}{bottom}\right) = \log \left(top\right) - \log \left(bottom\right) \)
Inverse: \( e^{\ln x} = x \), \( \ln \left(e^x\right)=x \)
Graphing
Graphing
x-intercept: \( (x_{int}, 0 ) \): Set \(y=0\) and solve for \(x\).
y-intercept: \( (0, y_{int} ) \): Set \(x=0\) and solve for \(y\).
Parabola Vertex: \( x=-\frac{b}{2a} \)
Function Transformations
Graph Up/Down Shift: \( f(x) \Longrightarrow f(x) \pm k \)
Graph Left/Right Shift: \( f(x) \Longrightarrow f(x \pm h) \)
Quadratics
Quadratic Formula: \( \displaystyle x=\frac{-b\pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a} \)

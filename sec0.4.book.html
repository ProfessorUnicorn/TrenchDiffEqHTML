Limits

Basic Limits \begin{align*} \lim_{x \to a} c = & c \\ \lim_{x \to a} x = & a \\ \end{align*} Limit Laws \begin{align*} \lim_{x \to a} \Big( c \cdot f(x) \Big) = & c \cdot \lim_{x \to a} f(x) \\ \lim_{x \to a} \Big( f(x) + g(x) \Big) = & \lim_{x \to a} f(x) + \lim_{x \to a} g(x) \\ \lim_{x \to a} \Big( f(x) \cdot g(x) \Big) = & \lim_{x \to a} f(x) \cdot \lim_{x \to a} g(x) \\ \lim_{x \to a} \left( \frac{ f(x) } { g(x) } \right) = & \frac{ \displaystyle \lim_{x \to a} f(x) }{ \displaystyle \lim_{x \to a} g(x) } \\ \lim_{x \to a} \Big( f^n(x) \Big) = & \Big( \lim_{x \to a} f(x) \Big)^n \\ \end{align*} Special Limits \begin{align*} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = & 1 \\ \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1-\cos x}{x} = & 0 \\ \end{align*} Infinite Limits \begin{align*} \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{1}{x} = & 0 \\ \lim_{x \to 0^+} \frac{1}{x} = & \infty \\ \lim_{x \to 0^-} \frac{1}{x} = & -\infty \\ \lim_{x \to \infty} e^x = & \infty \\ \lim_{x \to -\infty} e^x = & 0 \\ \end{align*}

Derivatives

Limit Definition \begin{equation*} f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h} \end{equation*} Basic Derivative Rules \begin{align*} \frac{d}{dx} \Big( c \Big) = & 0 \\ \frac{d}{dx} \Big( x \Big) = & 1 \\ \frac{d}{dx} \Big( c \cdot f(x) \Big) = & c \cdot f'(x) \\ \frac{d}{dx} \Big( f(x) + g(x) \Big) = & f'(x) + g'(x) \\ \end{align*} Advanced Derivative Rules \begin{align*} \frac{d}{dx} \Big( x^n \Big) = & nx^{n-1} \\ \frac{d}{dx} \Big( f(x) \cdot g(x) \Big) = & f(x) \cdot g'(x) + g(x) \cdot f'(x) \\ \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} \right) = & \frac{g(x) \cdot f'(x) - f(x) \cdot g'(x)}{ g^2(x) } \\ \frac{d}{dx} \bigg( f \Big( g(x) \Big) \bigg) = & f' \Big( g(x) \Big) \cdot g'(x) \\ \end{align*}

Trig Derivatives \begin{align*} \frac{d}{dx} \Big( \sin x \Big) = & \cos x \\ \frac{d}{dx} \Big( \cos x \Big) = & -\sin x \\ \frac{d}{dx} \Big( \tan x \Big) = & \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{\sin x}{ \cos x} \right) \\ \end{align*}

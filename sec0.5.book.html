0.5 Calculus II
Taylor Series
Taylor Series: \begin{equation*} \boxed{ f\left(x\right) = f\left(a\right) +f^{'}\left(a\right)\left(x-a\right) +\frac{f^{''}\left(a\right)}{2!} \left(x-a\right)^2 +\frac{f^{'''}\left(a\right)}{3!} \left(x-a\right)^3 +\frac{f^{^{IV}}\left(a\right)}{4!} \left(x-a\right)^4 + \cdots } \end{equation*}
Maclaurin Series \begin{equation*} \boxed{ f\left(x\right) = f\left(0\right) +f^{'}\left(0\right)\left(x\right) +\frac{f^{''}\left(0\right)}{2!} \left(x\right)^2 +\frac{f^{'''}\left(0\right)}{3!} \left(x\right)^3 +\frac{f^{^{IV}}\left(0\right)}{4!} \left(x\right)^4 + \cdots } \end{equation*} Taylor Series centered at 0 is called the Maclaurin Series

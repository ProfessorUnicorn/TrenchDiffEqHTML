38.
Suppose \(a,b\), and \(c\) are constants and \(a\ne 0\). Let \[ y_1={\cal L}^{-1}\left (as+b\over as^2+bs+c\right )\mbox {\quad and \quad } y_2={\cal L}^{-1}\left (a\over as^2+bs+c\right )\] Show that \[ y_1(0)=1,\quad \dot{y_1}(0)=0\quad \mbox{ and } \quad y_2(0)=0,\quad \dot{y_2}(0)=1\] Hint: Use the Laplace transform to solve the IVPs
\begin {eqnarray*} a\ddot{y}+b\dot{y}+cy&=&0, \quad y(0)=1,\quad \dot{y}(0)=0 \\ a\ddot{y}+b\dot{y}+cy&=&0, \quad y(0)=0,\quad \dot{y}(0)=1 \end {eqnarray*}

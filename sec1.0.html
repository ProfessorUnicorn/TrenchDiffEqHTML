CHAPTER 1
Introduction

IN THIS CHAPTER we begin our study of differential equations.

SECTION 1.1 presents examples of applications that lead to differential equations.

SECTION 1.2 introduces basic concepts and definitions concerning differential equations.

SECTION 1.3 presents a geometric method for dealing with differential equations that has been known for a very long time, but has become particularly useful and important with the proliferation of readily available differential equations software.
